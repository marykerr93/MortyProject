With so many mysteries surrounding Morty Brown it is not surprising that only he knows the origins of his story. To us, his life began when he was abandoned in a park in Sarasota, FL. He was rescued by some friends, and placed under Tyler's care. At first, Morty was cautious. He had been burned before. It took some time for him to warm up, but he has grown into a beautiful, loving member of our family. This page reviews some of Morty Brown's likes, dislikes, his schedule, and how to contact him.
